How to Play

The goal is to convert a set of starting words into a set of goal words. Each word may only be used once. You have access to the following operations:

/ :

You may convert any word to its IPA form and vice versa. eg. "read" to /ri:d/ or /rɛd/ , and /rɛd/ to "read" or "red".

:

You may convert a word to any of its inflectional variants/other forms. eg. "read" can become "reading" or "reads". "I" can become "me", "my", or "mine".

/ :

You may delete any letters or phonemes. Eg. change "what" to "hat", or /θɪŋk/ to /θɪŋ/.

:

You may merge two words together. eg "thin" and "king" to "thinking".

:

You may split a word into two words, so long as the split can occur without any letters/sounds left over. So "thinking" to "thin" and "king" would be valid. The thing that is being split must also be a word. So "bighat" to "big" and "hat" is not valid because "bighat" is not a word.
